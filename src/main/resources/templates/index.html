About Us

We are creating 40+ video tutorials of 10-12 minutes each on nourishing a mother and a child in the first 1000 days of an infant’s life, i.e, from day 1 of pregnancy till a child’s 2nd birthday. Each video will be translated and dubbed in 22 officially recognized Indian languages to cover the entire Indian landscape. Through this series, the Health Spoken Tutorial project at IIT Bombay aims to reach out to scores of people for spreading awareness on nutrition and improve the quality of life in India and globally as well.
